About

I'm a fine-arts student fascinated by the space between traditional media and digital experimentation. My work moves between acrylics, watercolor, and illustration, always chasing a feeling rather than a likeness.

When I'm not in the studio you'll find me sketching strangers in cafés and posting works-in-progress to my Instagram.
